Explore wonders of solitude & wildness with Glamp Everest!
Glamer and comfort served exotically- In wild!

Experience a dramatic fusion of country side camping and luxurious comfort, blended together in our uniquely designed glamps, a quiet sky and starry nights, fine waterfront landscapes, an inspiring and creative common area, delicious in-house dining with music and solitary vibes, and artistically decorated private luxurious Glamps.

Our glamorous glamps claim all royal pleasures offered right into the heart of wild, away from conventional crowded getaway spots. A classic spacious bamboo washroom, comfortable linens and king-size beds, personalized decor, campfire, music, and most importantly a peace of mind- we have it all so that you can unplug and reconnect with your true-self. As is said- Nature heals and nature binds, come discover yourself!
